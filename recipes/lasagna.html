Lasagna

Description

This is a cheap, quick, and delicious take on a traditional lasagna. The cost savings come from using a Ragu base and Spaghetti-o hot dog bites.

My mother inspired this recipes as she's also cheap and quick.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Get the pasta in the pan
  2. Put the meat in the pasta
  3. Season the meat before or after Step 2
  4. Cover with cheese
  5. Cook it
