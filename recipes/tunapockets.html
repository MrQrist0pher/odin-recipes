Tuna Pockets

Description

This Tuna Pocket is inspired by Hot Pockets and Calzones. There definitely need to be more seafood options

Ingredients

  • Tuna Fish
  • White Bread
  • Cheese Slices

Steps

  1. Spread tuna on the bread
  2. Cover tuna with cheese
  3. Repeat steps 1-2 so you have two tuna breads covered with cheese
  4. Melt the cheese with whatever you can
  5. Smash the tuna breads together, cheese side against the other bread cheese side
  6. Enjoy
